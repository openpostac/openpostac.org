Open Post Academics

Our online book called “Leaving the Academy: Resources from Open Post Academics” is now available!!
In this book, we share the information from the first cohort of our Open Post Academic curriculum including notes from our calls, the exercises we did (plus a few more) as well as hundreds of links and resources to support people with a PhD who are thinking about leaving academia. We welcome feedback and additional resources.

Purpose

To empower people with a PhD to share their knowledge and skills more openly.


Objectives

  • To expand the network of participants to more people outside of academia.
  • For participants to understand their skill set and how to apply those abilities to projects outside of academia.
  • To create a project that shares those skills openly.


The Open Post Academic Mentorship Program which was incubated through the Mozilla Open Leaders program. All participants in our programs agree to abide by our Code of Conduct.
